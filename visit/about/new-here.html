Our faith is more than an idea

Our faith in who Jesus is should affect our thoughts, feelings, and actions. We express Gods love for others by meeting not just their spiritual needs, but their physical needs as well. In a world filled plagued with poverty, racism, spiritual emptiness, disease, and natural disasters, we have the opportunity to reach out and make a difference in the lives of others. This can be in your neighborhood, in your city, or around the world!
